Using Dipthongs as Grammar (Short)

In the previous lesson, you learned the rest of the Ceronec letters. This is a short lesson, but it is important to understand different Ceronec texts and literature. In Ceron, we use commas, periods, brackets etc but they are not represented in the way most people are used to. Here is a list of common gramatical letters in Ceron and their English equivalent:

eur= , (comma)

aer= . (period)

ue= ! (exclamation mark)

wa= ?* (question mark)

*(This word denotes questions in general)

It's not extremely important to remember these letters' functions although they might help you better understand complex Ceronec pieces of writing in the future. Also note that in speaking, you musn't say these words out loud, just like how you wouldn't say 'comma' outloud in English.
