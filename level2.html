Level 2 - Basic Grammar (5-17)

  • Level 2 - Lesson 5

    • In this lesson, you will learn basic greeting words as well as the word order used in Ceron. You will also learn how to use the verb 'to be' to create simple sentences such as "I am a student", "This is a book" and "This house is big" as well as the different classifications of adjectives in Ceron.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 6

    • In this lesson, you will learn how to use other verbs in Ceron as well as their classifications. You will also learn how to use some of the most important prepostions and particles in Ceron.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 7

    • In this lesson you will learn the basic pronouns in Ceron.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 8

    • In this lesson you will learn how to use the negative form as well as how to express “to have”. There will also be more information on the verb “to like which is both an adjective and verb in Ceron.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 9

    • In this lesson, you will learn how to create the past, present and future tense in Ceron. You will also learn how to say that you have more or less of something.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 10

    • In this lesson you will learn the many different ways of asking questions in Ceron. You will also learn how to say 'with' or 'using'.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 11

    • In this lesson, you will learn the months, seasons and days in Ceron and how they differ from the English ones. In the end, you will be able to say things like "Summer is so nice" and "I hate winter". You will also learn colors in Ceron.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 12

    • Small but important words like Well, Not Well, More, Less, And etc

  • Level 2 - Lesson 13

    • More small but important words like Because, But, Also, Now etc

  • Level 2 - Lesson 14

    • In this lesson you will learn how to use adverbs as well as how to express 'wants' in Ceron so you can create sentences like "I want to eat" or "I want to go home".

  • Level 2 - Lesson 15

    • In this lesson you will learn how to express before and after as well as how to express doing two things at the same time.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 16

    • In this lesson you will learn how to express probability of an action happening as well as how to use one of the most basic functions of -dacde.

  • Level 2 - Lesson 17

    • In this lesson you will learn the numbers and how to count time, give directions as well as how to qoute people or yourself. You will also learn how to express if an action will happen.
