Level 1 - The Absolute Basics (1-4)

  • Level 1 - Lesson 1

    • In this lesson you learn the Alphabet used in Ceron which will allow you to read words and sentences throughout the lessons. This lesson is the foundation for all other lessons as from this point on the IPA translation will not be featured alongside words.

  • Level 1 - Lesson 2

    • In this lesson you will learn the more complicated letters in Ceron which repersent two sounds. You will also learn irregular pronounciation where words are not pronounced the way they are spelt.

  • Level 1 - Lesson 3

    • In this lesson you will learn how to use dipthongs as written grammar. (Yes written, Ceron does not use commas, periods etc, instead we use letters (diphthongs) to represent them)

  • Level 1 - Lesson 4

    • In this lesson you will learn how many words in Ceron are constructed. Although this is not necessary for a beginner, it will allow you to further understand the Ceronec Language.
