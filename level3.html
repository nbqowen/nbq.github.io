Level 3 - Lower Intermediate Grammar (18-30)

  • Level 3 - Lesson 18

    • In this lesson you will learn how to express the 'need' of something. You will learn how to create sentences like "I need to go home" and "I need to leave"

  • Level 3 - Lesson 19

    • In this lesson you will learn how to say you must, should or have to do something. You will also learn how this differs from 'need' in Ceron. I'll also go over how you express sentences where you think something happened.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 20

    • In this lesson you will learn how to express before, after and soon. You will also learn how to express from and until (a time).

  • Level 3 - Lesson 21

    • The many more meanings of dacde.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 22

    • In this lesson you will learn how to express going somewhere in order to do something or doing something in order to acheive something.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 23

    • In this lesson you will learn the process of nominalization in Ceron by adding 'a' after verbs.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 24

    • In this lesson you will learn the other ways of nominalizing words using 'gu' and 'u'.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 25

    • In this lesson you will learn how to express words and concepts like when, once, ever since, as etc.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 26

    • In this lesson you will learn the other ways in which you can express the future tense and learn how they differ from the rest.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 27

    • Sentence endings!

  • Level 3 - Lesson 28

    • In this lesson I will go in depth on the usage of ca, la (there), ca a and la a.

  • Level 3 - Lesson 29

    • In this lesson you will learn the imperative tense in Ceron aswell as how to express things like "I am optimistic about..." using "myasara a komad"

  • Level 3 - Lesson 30

    • In this lesson, I will quickly go over how to express things like 'may I' or 'can I' in Ceron.
