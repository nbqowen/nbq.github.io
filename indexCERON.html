Introduction to Ceron

The purpose of this website is to serve as a gateway for other people (as well as me) to learn Ceron. Ceron is a conlang which I have been developing for around 4 years now and just recently got a standardized script for. The website is divided into 7 levels or units to allow for easier navigation. Since I myself forget certain grammar principles sometimes, all information regarding grammar will be included in the ‘About Ceron’ tab. The levels range from basic grammar to advanced grammar as well as colloquialisms and a large list of vocabulary (around 2,300 with more being added frequently.) Audio recordings can be found beside each word or sentence in Ceron to allow for consistent pronunciation since IPA transcriptions will not be included. You’ll also find that at the end of lessons, novels, short stories or TV-show episodes with Ceronec subs will be included to allow certain vocabulary and grammar principles to stick in your head and also serve as an encouragement. Thank you for choosing to learn Ceron and feel free to email me to ask questions! (contact info under about me.)
